Description

A hearty and flavorful Italian dish, lasagna is a layered casserole made with pasta, cheese, and meat or vegetable filling. This recipe uses a simple meat sauce and ricotta cheese filling, making it a comforting and delicious meal for any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 12 Chicken noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain any excess fat and add the marinara sauce to the pan.
  3. In a bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, and grated Parmesan cheese. Set aside.
  4. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  5. Layer 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, spreading a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  6. Repeat layering the sauce, noodles, and cheese mixture until all ingredients have been used. Top with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake the lasagna in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the edges are golden brown.
